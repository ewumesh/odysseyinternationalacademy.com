Quality Education

A quality education is one that focuses on the whole child—the social, emotional, mental, physical, and cognitive development of each student regardless of gender, race, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, or geographic location. It prepares the child for life, not just for testing.

Top Level

Higher education is tertiary education leading to award of an academic degree. Higher education, also called post-secondary education, third-level or tertiary education, is an optional final stage of formal learning that occurs after completion of secondary education.

Quality Meeting

Quality Meetings are conducted to review the Quality Reviews that have been generated. The minutes (summary) of these meetings would include the discussion on the various Quality Reviews.
